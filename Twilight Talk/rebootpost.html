Twilight Zone Reboot

June 26, 2018 by Douglas Hawkes

Reboot is right! There is another reboot in the making and it's being led by none other than Jordan Peele, the writer/director of the critcally acclaimed movie 'Get Out'.

If this happens it will be the third revival in the last thirty years. Some months ago CSB announced that a reboot was going to happen and will also collaborate with Simon Kinberg and Marco Ramirez and producers such as Peele's Monkeypaw Productions. I feel like this will probably be the best revival we're going to get and the last one as well but I absolutely hope third times the charm.

Will Jordan Peele Fit?

Peele happens to be a fan of another anthology show that was inspired by the Twilight Zone, 'Black Mirror' so it seems he would be the ideal choice. If you don't know Peele's work, he also does comedy with Michael Keegan on the 'Key and Peele' and if you haven't seen 'Get Out' yet than please do.

Even the co-creator of 'Black Mirror' has said that he is looking forward to the reboot from Peele and if anyone should do it then it's him. I feel like he would bring an onslaught of horror and tie it in with a classic twilight twist in the end to wrap everything up like it should be.

Is it time?

Peele has stated, “Too many times this year it’s felt we were living in a twilight zone,” and "I can’t think of a better moment to reintroduce it to modern audiences". He is referring to the Trump era we are living in which feels surreal which makes it feel right on time. The actual date hasn't been confirmed yet but hopefully this winter, or 2019 at the latest.
